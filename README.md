📊 E-commerce Sales Analysis using Python
📌 Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce retail dataset using Python, Pandas, and Plotly to uncover sales and profit trends across multiple business dimensions. The analysis focuses on monthly performance, product categories, customer segments, and profitability to generate actionable business insights.

🎯 Problem Statement

The project addresses the following business questions:

Calculate monthly sales and identify the highest and lowest sales months.
Analyze sales across product categories.
Analyze sales across sub-categories.
Determine monthly profit trends and identify the most profitable month.
Compare profit across categories and sub-categories.
Analyze sales and profit by customer segment.
Calculate the Sales-to-Profit Ratio for each customer segment.
